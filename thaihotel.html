Night Hotel Bangkok - Sukhumvit 15

Night Hotel Bangkok - Sukhumvit 15 is a 4-star boutique hotel strategically located in the center of Bangkok, on Soi Sukhumvit 15, Watthana district. The hotel offers a comfortable and stylish stay, perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

Address: 10 Sukhumvit Soi 15, Kloengtoey Nua, Wattana, Bangkok 10110 , Thailand.
