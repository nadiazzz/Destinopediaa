Wat Arun

Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is one of Bangkok's most iconic landmarks, situated on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River in the Bangkok Yai district. Named after Aruna, the Hindu god of dawn, the temple is renowned for its stunning riverside location and its central prang (spire), which is intricately decorated with colorful porcelain and seashells.

Address: 158 Wang Doem Road, Wat Arun Subdistrict, Bangkok Yai District, Bangkok 10600, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 17:30
Price: Start from 200 THB (Adult) / Free (Children)

Wat Pho

Wat Pho, officially known as Wat Phra Chetuphon Wimon Mangkhalaram Rajwaramahawihan, is one of Bangkok's most revered temples. Renowned for housing the magnificent Reclining Buddha, this temple is a cornerstone of Thai cultural and religious heritage.

Address: 2 Sanam Chai Road, Phra Borom Maha Ratchawang, Phra Nakhon District, Bangkok 10200, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 17:00
Price: Start from 300 THB (Adult) / Free (Children)

Grand Palace

The Grand Palace in Bangkok is a magnificent complex that has been the heart of Thailand's royal history since 1782. Situated along the Chao Phraya River, this 218,400-square-meter site encompasses a series of opulent buildings, courtyards, and gardens, reflecting the grandeur of Thai architecture and the nation's rich cultural heritage.

Address: Na Phra Lan Road, Phra Nakhon District, Bangkok 10200, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 08:30 - 15:00
Price: Start from 500 THB (Adult) / Free (Children)

Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden

Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden is a renowned botanical garden located near Pattaya, Thailand. Spanning over 500 acres, it offers a blend of meticulously landscaped gardens, cultural shows, and recreational activities, making it a must-visit attraction for nature enthusiasts and families alike.

Address: 34/1 Moo 7, Na Chom Thian, Sattahip District, Chon Buri 20250, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 18:00
Price: Start from 349 THB (Adult) / Free (Children)

Phuket Fantasea

Phuket FantaSea is a renowned cultural theme park located in Kamala Beach, Phuket, Thailand. Spanning 60 acres, it offers a unique blend of traditional Thai culture and modern entertainment, making it a must-visit attraction for tourists.

Address: 99 Moo 3, Kamala, Kathu District, Phuket 83150, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 07:30 - 23:30
Price: Start from 1.800 THB (Adult) / 1.800 THB (Children)

Chiang Mai Night Market

Chiang Mai Night Bazaar is a vibrant and bustling market that comes alive every evening in the heart of Chiang Mai, Thailand. It's renowned for its extensive array of goods, delectable street food, and lively atmosphere, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

Address: 41 Changklan Road, Tambon Chang Khlan, Amphoe Mueang Chiang Mai, Chiang Mai 50100, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 17:00 - 00:00
Price: Free to Walk Around

Chatuchak Weekend Market

Chatuchak Weekend Market, also known as JJ Market, is one of the world's largest and most diverse weekend markets, located in Bangkok, Thailand. Spanning over 35 acres, it boasts more than 15,000 stalls divided into 27 sections, offering everything from clothing and accessories to antiques and food.

Address: Kamphaeng Phet 2 Road, Chatuchak, Bangkok 10900, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 18:00 (Saturday & Sunday)
Price: Free to Walk Around

Pattaya Beach

Pattaya Beach is a vibrant coastal destination located in Chon Buri Province, Thailand. Stretching approximately 3 kilometers along the Gulf of Thailand, it's renowned for its lively atmosphere, water activities, and proximity to various entertainment venues.

Address: Chon Buri, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Free

Khao Yai National Park

Khao Yai National Park is Thailand's first national park, established in 1962, and is renowned for its rich biodiversity, lush landscapes, and diverse wildlife. Spanning across Nakhon Ratchasima, Prachinburi, Saraburi, and Nakhon Nayok provinces, the park offers visitors a chance to experience nature through various activities and attractions.

Address: P.O. Box 9, Mu Si Sub-district, Pak Chong District, Nakhon Ratchasima Province 30130, Thailand.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 400 THB (Adult) / 200 THB (Children)
