Pho 2000

Phở 2000 is a renowned Vietnamese restaurant in Ho Chi Minh City, famously known for serving former U.S. President Bill Clinton during his visit in 2000. Situated near the bustling Bến Thành Market, the restaurant offers a clean and modern dining environment with ample seating.

Address: 1–3 Phan Chu Trinh Street, Bến Thành Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 07:00 - 22:00
Price: Start from 40.000 VND

Quan An Ngon

Quán Ăn Ngon is a renowned Vietnamese restaurant located in Hanoi's Hoan Kiem District. Set in a charming colonial villa, the restaurant offers a unique dining experience where guests can enjoy authentic Vietnamese dishes from the north, central, and south regions of the country.

Address: 18 Phan Bội Châu Street, Cửa Nam Ward, Hoàn Kiếm District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 06:30 - 22:00
Price: Start from 50.000 VND

Bún Chả Hương Liên

This modest, multi-story eatery offers a quintessential Hanoi dining experience with its stainless steel tables and plastic stools. The menu features the traditional bún chả, a dish comprising grilled pork served over rice noodles with fresh herbs and a flavorful dipping sauce.

Address: 24 Lê Văn Hưu Street, Phan Chu Trinh Ward, Hai Bà Trưng District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 10:00 - 21:00
Price: Start from 40.000 VND

The Hanoi Social Club

It offers a relaxed atmosphere adorned with vintage furniture, local artwork, and lush greenery. The café is a cultural hub, hosting live acoustic music, poetry readings, and open mic nights, making it a favorite among locals, expats, and travelers seeking a creative and communal space.

Address: 6 Hội Vũ Street, Hoàn Kiếm District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 22:00
Price: Start from 60.000 VND

Cộng Cà Phê

Established in 2007 in Hanoi, the café draws inspiration from the subsidized economy era of Vietnam, evident in its vintage décor featuring military-green hues, antique furnishings, and propaganda-style artwork.

Address: 152D Triệu Việt Vương Street, Hai Bà Trưng District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 08:00 - 23:00
Price: Start from 25.000 VND

Bánh Mì Phượng

Bánh Mì Phượng is a renowned sandwich shop located in Hoi An, Vietnam. Established in the 1990s, it gained international fame after being featured by the late chef Anthony Bourdain, who praised its offerings as some of the best bánh mì in the country.

Address: 2B Phan Châu Trinh Street, Cẩm Châu Ward, Hội An City, Quảng Nam Province, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 06:30 - 19:30
Price: Start from 15.000 VND

La Verticale

La Verticale is a renowned French-Vietnamese fusion restaurant in Hanoi, Vietnam, established by Chef Didier Corlou. Housed in a charming 1930s French colonial villa, the restaurant offers a blend of French gastronomy with Vietnamese spices and ingredients.

Address: 19 Ngô Văn Sở Street, Hoàn Kiếm District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 11:30 - 14:30, 18:00 - 22:00
Price: Start from 1.505.000 VND

Nhà Hàng Ngon

Nhà Hàng Ngon (also known as Quán Ăn Ngon) is a celebrated Vietnamese restaurant chain with locations in both Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Renowned for offering a comprehensive selection of traditional Vietnamese dishes from various regions, it provides an authentic culinary experience in a charming setting.

Address: 18 Phan Bội Châu Street, Cửa Nam Ward, Hoàn Kiếm District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 10:00 - 22:00
Price: Start from 50.000 VND

Nhà Hàng Ngon

Established in 1979 by Nguyễn Trọng Thìn, this family-run eatery has maintained its reputation by serving a single signature dish: phở bò tái lăn—stir-fried rare beef noodle soup. Unlike traditional pho, where raw beef is cooked in the broth.

Address: 13 Lò Đúc Street, Ngô Thì Nhậm Ward, Hai Bà Trưng District, Hanoi, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 06:30 - 21:00
Price: Start from 80.000 VND

Sadec Quan

The restaurant is known for its signature dishes like bánh tằm bì xíu mại (silkworm-shaped rice noodles with shredded pork skin and pork meatballs) and hủ tiếu Sa Đéc (Sa Đéc-style rice noodle soup). The ambiance reflects a nostalgic, rustic charm reminiscent of southern Vietnam's countryside.

Address: 154 Nguyễn Đình Chiểu Street, Ward 6, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
Operational Hours: 11:30 - 22:00
Price: Start from 40.000 VND
